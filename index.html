Understanding Tooth Whitening

What Causes Tooth Discoloration?

Tooth discoloration occurs due to a variety of reasons:

- Diet: Frequent consumption of coffee, tea, wine, and certain fruits can stain your teeth.

-Tobacco Use: Smoking or chewing tobacco significantly contributes to yellowing teeth.

- Medications: Certain medications can cause tooth discoloration as a side effect.

- Aging: Over time, the outer layer of enamel wears down, revealing the yellowish dentin beneath.

- Poor Oral Hygiene: Inadequate brushing and flossing can lead to plaque and tartar buildup, which stains teeth.
